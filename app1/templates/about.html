About Desi Hands

Connecting Indian artisans with customers who love handmade products.

Our Story

Desi Hands was founded with a simple mission: to connect talented Indian artisans with customers who appreciate authentic handmade products. We believe in preserving traditional crafts while providing artisans with a sustainable livelihood.

What started as a small passion project has grown into a thriving marketplace where artisans from across India showcase their incredible craftsmanship. From intricate jewelry to beautiful home decor, every product tells a story of tradition, skill, and passion.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower local artisans and preserve India's rich cultural heritage by providing them with a platform to reach a global audience. We ensure fair compensation for their work and help them continue their traditional crafts for future generations.

We carefully curate each product to ensure authenticity, quality, and fair trade practices. When you shop with Desi Hands, you're not just buying a product - you're supporting a family, preserving a tradition, and contributing to sustainable livelihoods.
